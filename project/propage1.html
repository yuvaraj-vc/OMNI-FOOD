What we do ?

Four CORE reports we provide to our customers

01

Customer contribution report

This report provides a overall information about customer contribution, product wise customer contribution, Area wise product movement and Volume wise product sales from customer.

02

MR sales contribution report

This report provides information about the Medical Representative wise sales, Medical Representative wise sales from each stockiest and Medical Representative wise percentage contribution to the target in pool territory.

03

Product secondary sales report

This report helps to know Brand wise and SKU wise secondary sales, Monthly sales unit wise, value wise and total group volume sales.

04

Customer ranking profile

This report help the customer to know about MR wise customer contribution and Sorted Ranking of customer based on percentage of contribution.
